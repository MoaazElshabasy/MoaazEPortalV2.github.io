Climate Change Awareness Video

During a school competition for the Cambridge "Your World" Competition. A team of students came together to research climate change, including factors that catalyze it and factors we can do to decrease it. After thorough research, the team developed a quick 3-minute video voiced by me to inform people quickly what they need to know about climate change. The video won first place in the school and was among the top 10 nationally despite not making it to the "Your World" Competition finals. Global change movements in Egypt used our video as a source of awareness for the people.
